Studio Egret West

Memorable places that surprise & delight

Scope
Strategy, Identity, Web
Client
SEW

Studio Egret West are a mix of urban designers, architects, and landscape architects designing memorable places with compelling narratives. Their places include Mayfield in Manchester, The Old Vinyl Factory in London, and Park Hill in Sheffield.

We worked with the SEW team for six months to develop a new brand strategy, tone-of-voice, brand identity, and website. The website’s launch acted as a celebration of the studio’s story-so-far and a starting gun for the next decade-long chapter ahead. It won the ‘Best Overall’ prize at the Archiboo Awards in 2020.
