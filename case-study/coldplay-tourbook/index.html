Coldplay

The Music of the Spheres World Tour is Coldplay’s ongoing eighth world concert tour. The band had not toured for their previous record, putting shows on hold until they could devise an environmentally friendly approach. According to a plan they developed for over two years, CO2 emissions are reduced by up to 50% compared to their previous world tour.
